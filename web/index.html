Steganography is used here to hide a text in a picture.
Wsteg is a simple and free tool to hide/reveal a text in/from a picture.

  • To hide a text in a picture, upload your picture, upload -or type in- your text and finally, choose "Hide"
  • To reveal the hidden text from a picture, upload your picture,then choose "Reveal"
